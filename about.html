ABOUT SMILE FOR MILES TRAVEL AGENCY

Smile for Miles is a travel agency that is based in Nairobi Kenya.The organization was founded back in 2019 with the aim of helping individuals book tours across the country in the comfort of their homes. As a company, we believe that some of the best ideas come when one is on vacation, and that is one of the main reasons why we offer our customers the best tours in Kenya.

We have several travel destinations that our customers can choose from locally, ranging from the Mara Safari, Kisumu Lakeside and the Sandy Coastal Beaches. We offer the best prices for the travel destination that our customers choose. Apart from that, we have a lot of discounts both in the peak and the offpeak seasons.

HISTORY

Smile for Miles Travel Agency was founded by a group of young individuals back in 2019. The company began as a small organization, that was made up of adventurers. The founders had travelled to many destinations in the past, both locally and internationally. They noticed the challenges that most individuals face; hence, they felt that coming up with a company would help solve some of those problems.
In 2019, the company operated only in Nairobi and Kisumu. However, in 2020, the Smile for Miles Travel Agency has expanded its operations, such that the customers can book any destination in the country.With increased experience in the tours and travel industry, we intend to expand our operations internationally.
